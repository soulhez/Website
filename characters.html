Caracters

  • Ninja Monkey

    Ninja Monkey, known in folklore as Saru Boba Junga, wanders the mountains and swamps defending the land from the nefarious blight of the evil Oni (dark spirits). Ninja Monkey's sure gonna lick 'em with his starfish shurikens!

    Ninja Monkey is chocolate coloured, so when he gets in a mess from guzzling cocoa it doesn't show. Pick up choccie bars for him for double points!

  • Obbly Bobbly

    Obbly Bobbly delights in whacky self-expression maaan. Too much time taken by tie-dying shirts taint Obbly Bobbly's ability to think things through, though! Life's too short for fussing and fighting anyway, thinks Obbly Bobbly.

    Obbly Bobbly loves to get high on a sugar rush. Collect the sweet stuff for double points!

  • Icey Cream

    Icey Cream is a friendly and animated Neapolitan. Some might say that's she's a little 'flakey', but her syrupy persona is a sure hit with the hundreds of thousands who make up the '99' per cent.

    Icey Cream has been trying to work out how to make a Baked Alaska for years without much success. Help her out by grabbing any bags of flour you see for double points!

  • Blob

    The mighty Blob's power lies in his ability to summon a series of delicious sidekicks to bolster his battles, from the bottom of his bulbous belly. Blob (and minions) form a symbiotic relationship. It's cute, really.

    Blob (and his minions) need to gather tonnes of energy in order to support so much activity. Grab sugar sacks for them and receive double points!

  • Max the Knight

    Max the Knight rides to the rescue with kitchen utensils in hand. With a flick of the whisk, Max will soon seive the day. When not lurking in the pantry, Max likes to wade the low tides, prentending to be a shark. He'd better pack some rust-eater!

    Max has perhaps been eating too many things lying around in the pantry, and now finds it difficult to squeeze into his armour. Score double points for butter so he can more easily slip it on!

  • Glogog

    Glogog's shaggy mane and maniacal grin betray a steely determination to fight anything that tries to get past him. Despite being a born goalie, Glogog's hare-brained attitude can sometimes cause him to lose his head.

    Glogog has a secret fascination with eggs - they're so shiny and smoothe compared to him. Help him out by scooping up any eggs you find for double points!
